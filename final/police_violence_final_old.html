Who are the Chicago police force?

The Chicago Police Department (CPD) killing of Laquan McDonald in 2014 led to a city-wide reconning with violence at the hands of the CPD. The Invisible Institute filed a lawsuit to obtain and release an unprecidented level of police department and civilian complaint data. The following analyses uses this data to better understand who makes up the police force, and the violence they perpetuate.
